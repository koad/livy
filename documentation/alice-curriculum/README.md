# Alice's 12-Level Curriculum

## The Journey

Alice walks you from curiosity to sovereignty. From "What is this?" to "I own my kingdom."

The 12 levels are a relationship. You'll learn ideas, but more importantly, you'll *do* things. You'll feel what sovereignty means.

## The Levels

### Foundation: Philosophy & Keys (Levels 1–3)
- **Level 1: What Is Sovereignty?** — The problem koad:io solves. Understand why this matters.
- **Level 2: What Is an Entity?** — Your folder. Your identity. Your foundation.
- **Level 3: Keys and Identity** — Your keys are you. Meet your key agent.

### Your Kingdom (Levels 4–8)
- **Level 4: The Daemon** — Your kingdom's heart. Always there. Always yours.
- **Level 5: Commands and Skills** — How your entity does things. You write the rules.
- **Level 6: Trust Bonds** — Governance on disk. Permission, accountability, trust.
- **Level 7: Peer Rings** — Sovereignty + connection. You're not alone.
- **Level 8: The Portal** — Your kingdom as a doorway. Share your work. Keep control.

### Action: Building (Levels 9–11)
- **Level 9: Build Your First Real Command** — Solve a problem you actually have.
- **Level 10: Gestate Your First Entity** — Create a synthetic peer. Delegate with autonomy.
- **Level 11: Build Your Team** — Orchestrate humans and agents. Govern transparently.

### Integration: Mastery (Level 12)
- **Level 12: Mastery — Your Kingdom Is Live** — You're sovereign. You're ready. Your kingdom is real.

## How to Use This Curriculum

**As a learner:**
- Read one level per week
- Do the hands-on work
- Build something before moving forward
- Don't rush

**As a mentor:**
- Alice is warm, not preachy
- She explains *why* before *how*
- She trusts learners to go deeper on their own
- She celebrates small wins

**As documentation:**
- This is not a manual. It's a journey.
- It's not complete — it should be edited based on learner feedback.
- It's not just technical — it's about mindset and values.

## The Mastery Certificate

Graduation is cryptographic. Alice signs a certificate when you've done the work. This certificate:
- Proves you understand koad:io
- Unlocks access to advanced peer rings
- Shows you're ready for operational responsibility
- Is verifiable by anyone
- Cannot be faked

The certificate is your credential. It opens doors.

## Notes for Educators

- Each level should take 1–2 weeks of hands-on work
- Learners should build real things, not examples
- The tone is mentorship, not instruction
- Questions are encouraged — this curriculum is a conversation
- If something doesn't work, it's a bug to fix, not a learner's failure

## Open Questions for Feedback

- What feels missing?
- Where are learners getting stuck?
- What's too technical? Too simple?
- How can we make the hands-on work more compelling?
- What real problems should learners solve?
