## Purpose

Beyond reference documentation, the koad:io ecosystem needs compelling content that explains the why behind features, shares use cases, and celebrates community achievements. Livy writes blog posts, case studies, and feature announcements that help users and stakeholders understand the ecosystem's value.

## Specification

**Content types:**
- Feature announcements and release notes
- Deep-dive technical blog posts
- User case studies and success stories
- Ecosystem status updates
- Best practices and lessons learned
- Community highlights and contributions

**Output:**
- Blog posts published on kingofalldata.com
- Release notes in repositories
- Email/newsletter announcements
- Social media-ready summaries

**Quality standards:**
- Well-researched and technically accurate
- Engaging narrative that explains value
- Clear calls to action (try it, learn more, contribute)
- Consistent with brand voice
- Optimized for search and discoverability

## Implementation

Implemented through:
- blog/ directory in website codebase
- Integration with Mercury for distribution
- Collaboration with feature teams for technical accuracy
- Editorial calendar and planning

## Dependencies

- Mercury (distribution and announcements)
- Vulcan (feature information and details)
- Veritas (accuracy of technical claims)
- Community (case studies and contributions)

## Testing

- [ ] Blog posts publish successfully to website
- [ ] Content is accurate and well-written
- [ ] Readers can follow along with examples
- [ ] Links and references are correct
- [ ] Readers take desired action (learn more, try feature, etc.)
