## Purpose

Users, integrators, and operators frequently encounter common questions and issues. Rather than having them search for scattered answers, Livy maintains a comprehensive FAQ that covers common scenarios, troubleshooting, and best practices based on real community feedback.

## Specification

**Content:**
- Frequently asked questions organized by topic
- Troubleshooting guides for common problems
- How-to guides for popular workflows
- Common mistakes and how to avoid them
- Best practices for different use cases
- Links to relevant documentation and references

**Output:**
- docs/faq/ section on kingofalldata.com
- Community wiki or discussion space
- Searchable, indexed for discoverability
- Regular updates from support feedback

**Topics:**
- Getting started questions
- Integration and API questions
- Operational and maintenance questions
- Troubleshooting and debugging
- Best practices and patterns
- Security and compliance questions

## Implementation

Implemented through:
- docs/faq/ with markdown files organized by category
- Integration with community discussion platforms
- Regular review of support tickets and community channels for new questions
- Updates when new features or gotchas emerge

## Dependencies

- Community feedback (users and operators)
- Veritas (accuracy of technical information)
- Support team (understanding of common issues)
- Relevant entity teams (for specialized Q&A)

## Testing

- [ ] FAQ covers major categories of questions
- [ ] Answers are accurate and complete
- [ ] Links to relevant documentation work
- [ ] New users can find answers to common questions
- [ ] Troubleshooting guides solve the described problems

## Status Note

Core capability - continuously improved based on community feedback and support tickets. New questions added regularly as they emerge.
